2. VISA

Entry to Ethiopia requires Visa. Apply online Ethiopian eVISA.

Apply for either:

  • Conference Visa for USD $32
  • Tourist Visa for USD $52 (at least 3 days before travel)
  • Fill out form for Invitation Letter
